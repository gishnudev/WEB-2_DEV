LEGENDARY BODYBUILDERS

Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger is an Austrian and American actor, businessman, filmmaker, former politician, and former professional bodybuilder, known for his roles in high-profile action films. He served as the 38th governor of California from 2003 to 2011.

Ronald Dean Coleman is an American retired professional bodybuilder. Known as "The King", Coleman shares the all-time record for most Mr. Olympia titles at eight with Lee Haney.

Ramon Rocha Queiroz , commonly known as Ramon Dino or "O Dinossauro do Acre", is a Brazilian professional bodybuilder. He is the leading Classic Physique athlete in Brazil and the current Mr. Olympia runner-up in that category. He won the Arnold Classic Ohio in 2023. His weight limit in his category is 103 kilograms.
